One Dollar Game

Making a game with graphs

How to play:

Your goal is to get all nodes to a positive value, including 0.

By clicking on a node, you distribute 1 dollar to each connected node.

This game was inspired by a mathematical problem, which is not always solveable, so if you thing you can not solve it, just generate a new one and try again!
